Books​

Robotics Fundamentals​

  • "Modern Robotics" by Kevin Lynch and Frank Park

    • Comprehensive robotics theory
    • Free PDF available online
    • Book Website

  • "Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots" by Roland Siegwart

    • Mobile robotics focus
    • Sensor fusion and localization
    • MIT Press

Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence​

  • "Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, Dieter Fox

    • Probabilistic approaches to robotics
    • SLAM, localization, mapping
    • Essential for autonomous systems

  • "Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction" by Sutton and Barto

    • RL fundamentals
    • Free online version
    • Book Website

Programming & Tools​

  • "Programming Robots with ROS" by Morgan Quigley et al.

    • O'Reilly Media
