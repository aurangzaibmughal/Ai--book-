# Chapter 1: Introduction to AI

Welcome to your first chapter in the AI Textbook Platform! This chapter introduces you to the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence and sets the foundation for your learning journey.

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how we interact with technology and solve complex problems. In this chapter, you'll learn what AI is, its history, key concepts, and real-world applications.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this chapter, you will:

- Understand the definition and scope of Artificial Intelligence
- Learn about the history and evolution of AI
- Explore different types of AI systems
- Discover real-world applications of AI
- Understand the ethical considerations in AI development

## Prerequisites

This chapter assumes:
- Basic programming knowledge (any language)
- Familiarity with basic mathematics (algebra)
- Curiosity and willingness to learn!
