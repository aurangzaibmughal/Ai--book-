# Chapter 12: Training VLA Models

## Overview

Learn to train and fine-tune Vision-Language-Action models for custom robotic tasks and environments.

- VLA training pipelines

- Fine-tuning pre-trained models
- Multi-task learning for robotics
- Evaluation metrics for VLA models
- Distributed training strategies

## Prerequisites

- Completed Chapter 11: VLA Models
- PyTorch or TensorFlow experience
- Access to GPU resources
- Understanding of training workflows
